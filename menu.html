OUR COFFEE MENU

COFFEE

We use only organically grown, fair-trade coffee beans brewed with care by our expert baristas

Customize your drink with any flavor or milk

DRIP COFFEE

Your choice of either French or Italian roast. Freshly brewed in our urn or available as a pour over

Available in 12oz or 16oz sizes

ICED COFFEE

A blend of our French and Italian roasts brewed fresh brewed over ice. A bright and refreshing drink!

Available in 16oz or 20oz sizes

COLD BREW

Either our French or Italian roast slow brewed in cold water for 14 hours, making an exceptionally smooth, rich drink with high levels of caffeine and very low acidity.

Available in 16oz or 20oz sizes

ESPRESSO

Our espresso is a blend of South and Central American beans, dark roasted for a complex and full-bodied brew and thick crema.

Customize your drink with any flavor or milk

ESPRESSO

Sometimes it's all you need! Perfect for a quick energy boost or to power an all nighter.

Available as a single or double shot

Hot or over ice

ESPRESSO MACCHIATO

Espresso marked with a dollop of steamed milk foam. Bold and smooth.

Available as a single shot

AMERICANO

Just our espresso and water for a richer, deeper alternative to drip coffee.

Available hot in 12oz or 16oz

Available iced in 16oz or 20oz

LATTE

Simple espresso with steamed milk and a light layer of foam. Delicious plain or with any added flavor!

Available hot in 12oz or 16oz

Available iced in 16oz or 20oz

CAPPUCCINO

Espresso with half milk and half velvety foam. Perfect as a morning pick me up. Stronger than a latte and more balanced than a macchiato.

Available hot in 12oz only

FLAVORED LATTE

Go for a classic like vanilla or caramel, or mix it up with one of our many flavors and combinations!

Available hot in 12oz or 16oz

Available iced in 16oz or 20oz

MOCHA

Classic combination of chocolate and espresso. Make it extra sweet with white chocolate!

Available hot in 12oz or 16oz

Available iced in 16oz or 20oz

BLENDED LATTE

Espresso, milk and your choice of flavor blended with ice. Keep it plain, add vanilla, or make it a mocha! Perfect sweet treat on a hot day.

Available in 16oz or 20oz
