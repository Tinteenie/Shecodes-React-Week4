Our Journey

About our two nighthawks

Inspired by two friends who realized they need to have a space safe to hang out when all other local cafés and spots are closed by 10PM, owners Christine and Jennifer, created The Nighthawk's Café.


They created The NightHawk's Café as a safe place for other nighthawks and night owls to hang out in a city that actually does sleep.


The Nighthawk's café is a space space for all. It's a place for the early bird to grab a quick cup of coffee or tea and a pastry. It is a place for the nighthawks who have trouble sleeping to have a place to hang out. It's a place for the tired squirrels to just take a quick breather. Take a seat. Have a cup. Have a nightcap. Enjoy a bite to eat. It's okay. We got chu.
